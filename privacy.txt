Thank you for using Counter - MTG Life Tracker app. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

Information We Collect

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to the developer or third parties.

The app stores certain settings locally on your device in order to remember your preferences and improve usability. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Device Sensors

The app uses the device's ambient light sensor to detect changes in light levels in order to wake or activate the display during gameplay. Sensor data is processed only in real time on the device and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use advertising services, analytics tools, or other third-party tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
