Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Thank you for using Counter - MTG Life Tracker app. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

Information We Collect

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to the developer or third parties. No user accounts are required or created to use this app.

Local Data Storage

The app stores certain settings locally on your device in order to remember your preferences and improve usability. This information remains on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers.

Data Retention and Deletion (User Rights)
Because we do not transmit or store your personal information on our servers, we do not retain any of your personal data.

How long data is stored: The local settings and preferences mentioned above are retained on your device for as long as you have the app installed.

How to delete your data: You can delete all locally stored app data at any time by uninstalling the app from your device, or by clearing the app's data/storage within your device's system settings. Because no data is sent to the developer, there is no need to submit a data deletion request to us.

Device Sensors

The app uses the device's ambient light sensor to detect changes in light levels in order to wake or activate the display during gameplay. Sensor data is processed only in real time on the device and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Donations

The app may include a link to an external donation page to support development. If you choose to follow this link, you will be directed to a third-party website. Any information you provide on that site is governed by that service's own privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use advertising services, analytics tools, or other third-party tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
